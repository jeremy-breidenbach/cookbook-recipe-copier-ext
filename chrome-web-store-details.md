# Chrome Web Store Details

## Title
Cookbook Recipe Copier

## Summary
Copy a recipe from a URL and paste into Create My Cookbook

## Detailed Description
Cookbook Recipe Copier let's you easily copy recipe details from online recipes into your createmycookbook.com cookbook (Cookbook Recipe Copier is a third-party extension and is not affiliated with Create My Cookbook). 

### How to Use the Cookbook Recipe Copier
* In your createmycookbook.com account, open your cookbook and then open the Recipe Box
* Click Add Recipes and choose "I'll Type It"
* While the New Recipe form is open click the Cookbook Recipe Copier extension icon to show a popup where you can enter the URL of a recipe to copy
* Click the Copy Recipe button in the popup to copy the recipe details into the recipe form

### Cookbook Recipe Copier Options
To change the settings, right click on the extension icon and click Options.
Available Options:
* Download Recipe Image
* Paste Author into Recipe
* Paste Nutrition into Recipe Notes
* Paste URL into Recipe Notes
